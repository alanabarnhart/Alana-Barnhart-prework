About Me

Welcome to my website! My name is Alana Barnhart and I am a recent graduate of St. Olaf College (um ya ya!). In my free time, I love to bake, go for long bike rides, and play games with family and friends. Since graduating from school, I have enjoyed having extra time for all of these activities.

One of the recipes I have been trying to perfect is a replication of the Cappuccino Muffins from the St. Olaf cafeteria. These are my all time favorite because they are soft in the middle and crunchy on top, with the perfect hint of espresso, and, of course, plenty of chocolate chips. I have also gotten to explore some of the bike trails around my house. From where I live, I can get on trails that go almost anywhere - to downtown Minneapolis, out to Lake Minnetonka, around the chain of lakes, and plenty of local paths.

While I have loved this time of rest and freedom, I am looking forward to starting my post-grad professional career and I can't wait to see where life takes me next!
